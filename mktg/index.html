The Ideal One-Page Resume

Polished Resumes are generally a single page, that only includes information that is relevant to a potential employer.


By cutting out the fluff and hyperbole, your resume will be easier and faster to read and digest.

Powered By Data

Resumes are still a crucial part of winning a new job. Polished uses the power of data to determine current job market trends, and determines which parts of your job history will be most interesting to employers.

Taleo Friendly

Taleo is the most common job board solution on the market for small to large companies.

Polished Resumes are perfectly formatted to upload into Taleo job boards. This will allow you to apply for jobs more rapidly, since your information will upload to Taleo without requiring corrections.
